Result Checking

Posted on 26-Nov-18 11:59 AM

We are happy to announce that parents can now check their children's term result in our website. 

How To Check Your Child result

  1. Click the login button
  2. Enter Your Child login  information
  3. Click on view result
  4. Select the term and the session to view the result
  5. View and Print result

If you need any help in checking your child result, please do well to contact us.
