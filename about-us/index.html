About Us


Who we are

Bubiy Academy was founded by Miss Ezeonwujito Ebube .A. and her Members on September 2019, managed by Miss Ezeonwujito Ebube .A. a seasoned educationist, The school was born out of the desire to provide qualitative education to nursery and primary pupils of school age irrespective of race, tribe, economic or cultural background. Its philosophy is based on the national philosophy of education, that is provisions of quality basic education, values and skills to all children within the primary school age, equipping them to furtherance of higher education.

Our Mission


To provide Access to all  children of primary school age irrespective of tribe, race, socio-economic and cultural background through the inculcation of relevant knowledge, value, competencies and skill that will prepare them for the next level of education and also equip them to be creative and critical thinkers

Our Vision


To be the best providers of qualitative and functional basic educational programs in Nigeria.
